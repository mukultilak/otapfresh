ONLINE TRAINING AND PLACEMENT

Network/Web based system that automates the repetitive tasks and ensures effective & secure communication between students and Training and Placement cell through e-mail and SMS gateways.

Services

To your service!

 

Automation

Automates the redundant process of the Training and Placement cell to save time and avoid complexity of activities needed to be carried out for Training and Placement management - eg. Report generation. It provides a user friendly experience to the client.

Portability

System can be accessed through network which makes it very much portable and convenient for students as well as Training and Placement Cell. Also ensures communication between Training and Placement Cell and students via email and SMS.

Security

User can access the system only after verification. Students and Staff have their own dashboards which can be accessed by the authenticated user only. Identity is checked before account verification and user is allowed to use the services.
